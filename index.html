What is this?

xkcd, I have found, is a limitlessly educational rabbit hole. I regularly find myself gazing at a comic, lacking comprehension and needing to google the many obscure references it makes. By trawling through Wikipedia and various other websites, I almost always learn something that is both interesting and entirely unnecessary to know. This app is an attempt at sharing that same joy with you.
